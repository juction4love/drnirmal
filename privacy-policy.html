Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the official portfolio and practice of Dr. Nirmal Lamichhane collects, uses, and protects your information. As a medical professional, Dr. Nirmal is committed to maintaining the highest standards of patient confidentiality and data security.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information necessary to provide medical consultation and surgical services:

  • Personal Information: Name, age, phone number, and email address provided during appointment booking.
  • Medical History: Clinical details, reports, and symptoms shared for diagnosis purposes.
  • Log Data: Anonymous data regarding website traffic (e.g., browser type) to improve user experience.

2. Doctor-Patient Confidentiality

Dr. Nirmal Lamichhane adheres to strict Doctor-Patient Confidentiality protocols.

  • Any medical information shared via this website, WhatsApp, or email is treated as privileged clinical data.
  • Your medical records are never shared with third-party marketing agencies.
  • Data is only shared with other medical professionals (e.g., pathologists or radiologists) if it is strictly necessary for your treatment and with your consent.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely for:

  • Scheduling appointments and surgeries.
  • Providing accurate urological diagnosis and treatment plans.
  • Communicating follow-up instructions or test results.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
