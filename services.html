Clinical Expertise & Procedures

Comprehensive surgical solutions for urological malignancies and complex pelvic conditions, backed by over two decades of international experience.

Urological Oncology

Dr. Lamichhane specializes in the surgical management of genito-urinary tract tumors. His primary focus is on delivering curative outcomes while preserving quality of life through advanced reconstruction techniques. He combines rigorous oncological principles with meticulous surgical skill to treat various malignancies.

  • Bladder Cancer: Expertise in Radical Cystectomy. Dr. Nirmal has a special interest in the "Studer Pouch" (Orthotopic Neobladder), a complex procedure that constructs a new bladder from the intestine, allowing patients to urinate normally.
  • Prostate Cancer: Performing Radical Prostatectomy for high-risk cases and managing active surveillance protocols for early-stage disease to minimize overtreatment while ensuring safety.
  • Penile & Testicular Cancer: Surgical management including partial or total penectomy and meticulous groin lymph node dissections to ensure complete cancer removal.

Endourology

Utilizing minimally invasive technology to treat urinary tract conditions without open incisions. These procedures offer faster recovery times and reduced hospital stays.

  • TURP: Transurethral Resection of the Prostate is the gold standard treatment for benign prostate enlargement (BPH), relieving urinary obstruction.
  • TURBT: Endoscopic removal of bladder tumors for both accurate diagnosis and staging of bladder cancer before definitive treatment.

Complex Reconstruction

Leveraging his fellowship training in Colorectal Surgery from the UK, Dr. Lamichhane performs complex pelvic surgeries that often require multi-organ resection.

  • Colorectal Surgery: Management of advanced rectal cancers and colectomies for patients with synchronous tumors or extensive disease.
  • Breast Oncology: Experienced in Sentinel Lymph Node Biopsy using dye-guided methods for precise breast cancer staging.
  • Tumor Boards: Active involvement in multidisciplinary planning for difficult cases.
