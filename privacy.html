Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the official portfolio of Dr. Nirmal Lamichhane (accessible via drnirmal.com.np) handles information when you visit the site or contact us.

1. Information We Collect

We do not use tracking software to identify you personally. However, information may be collected in the following ways:

  • Contact Inquiries: If you contact us via email or telephone provided on the site, we may collect your name, phone number, and clinical details to assist with your medical inquiry.
  • Log Files: Like most websites, we use standard log files that record IP addresses, browser types, and timestamps for security and maintenance purposes.

2. Medical Confidentiality

As a medical professional, Dr. Nirmal Lamichhane adheres to strict patient confidentiality standards. Any medical information shared via private communication is treated as privileged clinical data and is not shared with third parties without your explicit consent, except as required by Nepalese law.

We may use cookies to improve your browsing experience (e.g., remembering your language preference). We may also use Google Analytics to understand website traffic, which collects anonymous data only.
