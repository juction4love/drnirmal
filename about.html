A Lifetime Dedicated to Oncology

Dr. Nirmal Lamichhane is a distinguished figure in the field of Nepalese oncology. His medical journey began with a Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS) from Shanghai Medical University in 1998. Driven by a passion for cancer care, he pursued a Master of Medicine in Clinical Oncology from Fudan University (2001) and later specialized further with a Magister Chirurgiae (M.Ch) in Urology from Kathmandu University in 2016. This robust academic foundation has enabled him to treat complex malignancies with precision and compassion.

Professional Leadership & Experience

His professional timeline is deeply rooted in the B.P. Koirala Memorial Cancer Hospital (BPKMCH). Joining as a Registrar in 2002, he ascended through the ranks to Junior Consultant and eventually Senior Consultant Surgeon. Dr. Lamichhane has held significant leadership responsibilities, notably serving as the Deputy Director from 2018 to 2020 and later as the Former Chairman of the Board of Directors. His vision helped shape the strategic growth of the institution. Additionally, he shared his extensive expertise as an Assistant Professor at the College of Medical Sciences (2012-2016).

Global Training & Fellowships

To bring world-class surgical techniques to Nepal, Dr. Lamichhane has completed extensive international training:

  • USA (2006): Completed the prestigious Saudavar Fellowship in Surgical Oncology at Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center (New York).
  • USA (2003): Specialized training in Sentinel Lymph Node Biopsy at the H. Lee Moffitt Cancer Center (Florida).
  • UK (2010): Fellowship in Colorectal Surgical Oncology sponsored by the Royal College of Surgeons of Edinburgh.

Memberships & Affiliations

Dr. Lamichhane is an active leader in the medical community. He is a Life Member of the Nepal Medical Association and the SAARC Federation of Oncologists. On the global stage, he maintains membership with the International Union Against Cancer (UICC) and the American Society of Clinical Oncology (ASCO), ensuring he stays updated with the latest advancements in cancer research and surgical innovation.
