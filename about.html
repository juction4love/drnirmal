Professional Milestones

Dr. Nirmal Lamichhane joined B.P. Koirala Memorial Cancer Hospital in 2002. Over two decades, he has held major leadership roles that shaped regional cancer care:

Former Chairman: Led the Board of Directors at BPKMCH, managing national oncology policies.

Former Deputy Director (2018–2020): Oversaw hospital clinical operations and administrative growth.

Academic: Former Assistant Professor of Surgery at the College of Medical Sciences, Bharatpur.

International Training

His surgical expertise is backed by fellowships at the world's leading cancer centers:

  • USA: Saudavar Fellowship at Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center (New York).
  • USA: Sentinel Node Biopsy training at H. Lee Moffitt Cancer Center (Florida).
  • UK: Colorectal Surgical Oncology Fellowship, Royal College of Surgeons of Edinburgh.
