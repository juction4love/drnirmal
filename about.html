Professional Milestones

Dr. Nirmal Lamichhane joined the B.P. Koirala Memorial Cancer Hospital (BPKMCH) in 2002, rising through the ranks from Registrar to Senior Consultant. Throughout his distinguished tenure, he has held significant administrative and leadership responsibilities that have shaped cancer care in the region:

Former Chairman of the Board of Directors Led the strategic governance and policy framework of BPKMCH to improve oncology services nationwide.
Former Deputy Director (2018–2020) Managed hospital operations, clinical services, and administrative coordination during a period of hospital expansion.
Academic Leadership Served as an Assistant Professor of Surgery at the College of Medical Sciences, Bharatpur, mentoring the next generation of Nepalese surgeons.

International Training & Clinical Excellence

Dr. Lamichhane’s expertise is backed by prestigious global training and fellowships at world-renowned medical institutions:

United States of America Saudavar Fellowship in Surgical Oncology at Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center (New York) and Sentinel Lymph Node Biopsy training at H. Lee Moffitt Cancer Center (Florida).
United Kingdom Fellowship in Colorectal Surgical Oncology sponsored by the Royal College of Surgeons of Edinburgh.

Today, he remains a dedicated Senior Consultant Surgical Oncologist, focusing on advanced urological procedures, complex pelvic reconstructions, and maintaining a high standard of patient-centered care in Nepal.
