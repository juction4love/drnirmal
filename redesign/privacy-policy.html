Privacy Policy

Welcome to the digital portal of Dr. Nirmal Lamichhane. Your privacy and the security of your clinical data are our highest priorities. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information across our website and the Dr. Nirmal Android Application.

1. Information We Collect

To provide high-quality surgical oncology and patient management services, we may collect the following data:

  • Personal Identity: Name, age, gender, and contact details (for appointment booking).
  • Clinical Data: Patient history, surgical records, and prescriptions (stored securely within the Android App).
  • Health Metrics: Daily anxiety levels and symptom logs (Anxiety Tracker module).

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used specifically for clinical purposes:

  • To maintain accurate patient records for surgical follow-ups.
  • To visualize anxiety trends and mental health spikes for specialized care.
  • To provide informational AI assistance regarding surgical procedures.

3. Data Security & Encryption

As a specialized surgical portal, we implement industry-leading security measures:

  • Encryption at Rest: All sensitive health data (clinical notes, anxiety logs) is stored using AES-256 encryption within the Android application.
  • Local Storage: Clinical data is stored on-device in a secure database and is not shared with third-party servers without explicit clinical authorization.
  • No External Tracking: We do not sell or share patient data with advertisers or analytics brokers.

4. User Rights & Data Deletion

You have full control over your information:

  • Access: You can view all your health records and anxiety logs within the app at any time.
  • Deletion: You may request the deletion of your account or clear your clinical data from the app settings.

5. Google Play Data Safety

The Dr. Nirmal Android Application is fully compliant with Google Play Health Data policies. We collect minimal information required for clinical monitoring and implement hardware-backed security for all sensitive fields.
