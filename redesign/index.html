About Dr. Nirmal Lamichhane

Senior Consultant Surgeon and Cancer Specialist at BPKMCH. Dr. Nirmal is a pioneer in modern cancer surgery in Nepal, with extensive training from Shanghai Medical University and clinical fellowships at Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center (New York) and the Royal College of Surgeons (UK).

His dedication to global medical standards brings world-class surgical oncology and research to the heart of Bharatpur.
