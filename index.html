Dedicated to Advanced Urological & Cancer Surgery

Dr. Nirmal Lamichhane is a highly respected Senior Consultant Surgical Oncologist and Urologist based in Bharatpur, Nepal. With over 20 years of experience, he is a leader in the diagnosis and surgical management of complex cancers.


In his distinguished career, he has served as the Former Chairman of the Board of Directors at the B.P. Koirala Memorial Cancer Hospital (BPKMCH). During his leadership, he played a vital role in enhancing oncology services and strategic development for the nation's premier cancer center.


Specializing in Urological Oncology, Dr. Lamichhane is an expert in treating bladder, prostate, and kidney cancers. He is uniquely trained in advanced procedures such as the orthotopic neobladder (Studer Pouch) reconstruction, bringing international surgical standards to patients in Nepal.
