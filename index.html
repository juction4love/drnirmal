Historical Executive Summary

Dr. Nirmal Lamichhane stands as a cornerstone of urological oncology in Nepal. With a surgical career spanning over two decades, he has integrated elite international training with local clinical leadership.

His tenure as Chairman and Deputy Director at B. P. Koirala Memorial Cancer Hospital marks a period of significant institutional growth and modernization in oncological surgery protocols.
