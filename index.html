About Me

I am Dr. Nirmal Lamichhane. I am a Senior Consultant Surgeon specializing in Surgical Oncology with a sub-specialization in Urological Cancers (Onco-Urology). I currently serve at the B. P. Koirala Memorial Cancer Hospital (BPKMCH) in Chitwan, Nepal.

My Background: I previously had the honor of serving as the Chairman of the Board of Directors at BPKMCH, where I oversaw the hospital's executive governance and strategic direction.

With over two decades of surgical experience, I hold an M.Ch in Urology from Kathmandu University and a Master of Medicine in Clinical/Surgical Oncology from Fudan University, Shanghai. My international training includes prestigious fellowships at Memorial Sloan Kettering (USA), H. Lee Moffitt Cancer Center (USA), and the Royal College of Surgeons of Edinburgh (UK).

Clinical Expertise

Specialized care in complex oncological surgeries with a focus on urology.
