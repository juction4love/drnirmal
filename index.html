About Me

I am Dr. Nirmal Lamichhane, a Senior Consultant Surgeon specializing in Surgical Oncology with a sub-specialization in Onco-Urology.

With over two decades of experience, I currently serve at the B. P. Koirala Memorial Cancer Hospital (BPKMCH) in Chitwan, Nepal. I am dedicated to introducing advanced surgical techniques to Nepal, having completed fellowships in the USA and UK.
