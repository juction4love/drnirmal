Professional Biographical Abstract

Dr. Nirmal Lamichhane is a distinguished Senior Consultant Surgeon specializing in Surgical Oncology with a sub-specialization in Urological Cancers (Uro-Oncology).

Executive Summary

Dr. Nirmal Lamichhane stands as a prominent figure in the field of surgical oncology in Nepal, currently serving as a Senior Consultant Surgeon at the B. P. Koirala Memorial Cancer Hospital (BPKMCH).

Possessing over two decades of surgical experience, he obtained his Magister Chirurgiae (M.Ch) in Urology from Kathmandu University and M.S. Surgical Oncology from Fudan University, Shanghai.

He was awarded the Saudavar Fellowship at Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center (MSKCC) in New York and holds a Fellowship in Colorectal Surgery from the Royal College of Surgeons of Edinburgh.

He is a pioneer in performing radical prostatectomies and radical cystectomies, holding particular authority in reconstructive urology (Studer pouch neobladder).

Clinical Expertise

  • Genito-Urinary Tract Tumors: Bladder, Prostate, and Kidney cancers.
  • Surgical Procedures: Radical Prostatectomy, Cystectomy, TURP.
  • Reconstruction: Expert in Studer pouch neobladder reconstruction.
  • General Oncology: Mastectomies, Colectomies, and Groin Dissections.
