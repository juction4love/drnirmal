Vision & Professional Legacy

Dr. Nirmal Lamichhane stands as a pillar of oncology in Nepal, blending decades of experience with global medical standards. His journey is defined by a deep-seated commitment to transforming cancer care in the country. As the Former Chairman of the B.P. Koirala Memorial Cancer Hospital (BPKMCH), he led the institution through critical phases of growth, ensuring that patients across Nepal had access to life-saving surgical treatments and modernized oncology protocols. He has dedicated his life to bridging the gap between advanced medical research and accessible clinical practice in Bharatpur.

Advanced Surgical Innovation

Dr. Lamichhane’s expertise in Urological Oncology has redefined treatment for bladder and prostate cancers in Bharatpur. He is particularly recognized for his mastery of the Orthotopic Neobladder (Studer Pouch), a complex procedure where a new bladder is constructed from the patient’s own intestine. This ensures that patients, following a radical cystectomy, can lead a life with dignity and normal function. His approach integrates multidisciplinary care, combining surgical precision with the latest findings in tumor biology.

Furthermore, his tenure at Shanghai Medical University and Fudan University provided him with a unique perspective on clinical oncology, which he later refined through prestigious fellowships at Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center (USA) and H. Lee Moffitt Cancer Center (USA). Today, he continues to serve as a Senior Consultant Surgeon, mentoring the next generation of surgeons while maintaining an active clinical practice that prioritizes patient survival and quality of life above all else.
