Dr. Nirmal Lamichhane

Senior Consultant Surgeon | Surgical Oncology (Onco-Urology)

B. P. Koirala Memorial Cancer Hospital (BPKMCH)

Biography

Dr. Nirmal Lamichhane is a distinguished Senior Consultant Surgeon specializing in Surgical Oncology and Onco-Urology. With a career defined by clinical excellence and strategic leadership, he has made significant contributions to cancer care in Nepal.

Leadership & Governance
Demonstrating strong administrative capability, he served as the Chairman of the Board of Directors at B. P. Koirala Memorial Cancer Hospital (BPKMCH) from 2020 to 2023. During his tenure, he oversaw the hospital's strategic governance, driving initiatives to enhance patient care and institutional growth.

Education & Academic Background
His medical journey began with an MBBS from Shanghai Medical University, followed by a Master of Medicine in Clinical and Surgical Oncology from Fudan University. He further specialized with an M.Ch in Urology from Kathmandu University, solidifying his expertise in complex surgical procedures.

International Exposure
Dr. Lamichhane has enriched his practice through extensive global training. He completed prestigious International Fellowships at world-renowned institutions, including the Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center (USA), H. Lee Moffitt Cancer Center (USA), and the Royal College of Surgeons of Edinburgh (UK). These experiences have equipped him with advanced surgical techniques and a global perspective on oncology.

Fluent in Nepali, English, Chinese, and Hindi, he is dedicated to providing compassionate, evidence-based care to patients from diverse backgrounds.
