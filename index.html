Executive Summary

Dr. Nirmal Lamichhane

Dr. Nirmal Lamichhane is a highly experienced surgeon and medical leader based in Bharatpur, Chitwan. He currently serves as the Medical Director and Consultant Surgeon at National City Hospital and as a Consultant Urologist at B.P. Koirala Memorial Cancer Hospital (BPKMCH).

He specializes in Urology and Uro-oncology, providing both surgical and non-surgical treatments for complex conditions. With over two decades of experience, he combines academic excellence with clinical leadership to drive advancements in patient care in Nepal.

Dr. Lamichhane obtained his Magister Chirurgiae (M.Ch) in Urology from Kathmandu University and M.S. Surgical Oncology from Fudan University, Shanghai. He has further refined his skills through fellowships at world-renowned centers like Memorial Sloan Kettering (USA) and Moffitt Cancer Center (USA).

His expertise includes radical surgeries for prostate, bladder, and kidney cancers, as well as reconstructive urology procedures such as the Studer pouch neobladder. As Medical Director, he now leads strategic initiatives to enhance healthcare services at National City Hospital.
