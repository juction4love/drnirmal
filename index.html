About Me

I am Dr. Nirmal Lamichhane. I am a Senior Consultant Surgeon specializing in Surgical Oncology with a sub-specialization in Urological Cancers (Onco-Urology). I currently serve at the B. P. Koirala Memorial Cancer Hospital (BPKMCH) in Chitwan, Nepal.

I previously had the honor of serving as the **Chairman of the Board of Directors** at BPKMCH (2020-2023), where I oversaw the hospital's executive governance and strategic direction.
