Patient Education (FAQ)

What are the early signs of Prostate Cancer?

Symptoms often include frequent urination (especially at night), difficulty starting urination, or blood in the urine. Early screening is vital.

What is a Studer Pouch?

It is a form of orthotopic neobladder reconstruction. After removing the bladder due to cancer, a new bladder is created from the patient's own intestine, allowing normal urination.
