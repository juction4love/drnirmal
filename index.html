Dedicated to excellence in cancer care, Dr. Nirmal Lamichhane is a distinguished Senior Consultant Surgeon based in Bharatpur, Nepal. He currently serves as the Chairman of the Board of Directors at the prestigious B.P. Koirala Memorial Cancer Hospital (BPKMCH).

With over two decades of specialized experience, Dr. Lamichhane is a leader in Urological Oncology and complex pelvic surgeries. His expertise spans the diagnosis, staging, and surgical management of solid tumors, with a specific focus on bladder, prostate, and genito-urinary tract cancers.

Dr. Lamichhane combines world-class education from Fudan University and Kathmandu University with advanced international fellowships in the USA (Memorial Sloan Kettering, H. Lee Moffitt) and the UK. He is committed to bringing advanced surgical techniques, such as orthotopic neobladder reconstruction, to patients in Nepal.
