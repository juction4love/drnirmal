Professional Biography

Dr. Nirmal Lamichhane is a distinguished figure in the field of Surgical Oncology in Nepal. With an academic foundation from Fudan University (Shanghai) and Kathmandu University, he has served as a cornerstone for urological cancer management for over 20 years.

His career is defined by high-level governance as the former Chairman of the Board and Deputy Director at B. P. Koirala Memorial Cancer Hospital (BPKMCH), where he pioneered advanced surgical protocols and patient advocacy programs.
