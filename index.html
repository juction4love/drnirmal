Historical Executive Summary

Dr. Nirmal Lamichhane stands as a cornerstone of Surgical Oncology in Nepal. Integrating elite international training from the Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center (New York) and Fudan University (Shanghai) with local clinical leadership, he has transformed cancer care protocols at the national level.

As a former Chairman and Deputy Director of the B. P. Koirala Memorial Cancer Hospital (BPKMCH), his career is defined by academic precision, surgical innovation, and two decades of service to patients across the SAARC region.
