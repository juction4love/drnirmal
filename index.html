Biography & Qualifications

जीवनी तथा योग्यता

Dr. Nirmal Lamichhane is a dedicated Senior Onco-Urology Surgeon with over two decades of experience. He completed his medical education in China and returned to Nepal to pioneer advancements in surgical oncology at the B. P. Koirala Memorial Cancer Hospital. He previously led the hospital governance as Chairman.

Education

  • M.Ch (Magister Chirurgiae) Urology: Kathmandu University, Nepal (2016)
  • Master of Medicine (Clinical/Surgical Oncology): Fudan University Medical School, Shanghai, China (2001)
  • MBBS: Shanghai Medical University, China (1998)

International Fellowships

  • Colorectal Surgery: Royal College of Surgeons of Edinburgh, UK (2010–2011)
  • Surgical Oncology: Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center, USA (2006)

डा. निर्मल लामिछाने दुई दशकभन्दा बढी अनुभव भएका एक समर्पित वरिष्ठ अन्को-युरोलोजी सर्जन हुनुहुन्छ। चीनमा आफ्नो चिकित्सा शिक्षा पूरा गरेपछि, उहाँले बी.पी. कोइराला मेमोरियल क्यान्सर अस्पतालमा सर्जिकल अन्कोलोजीको क्षेत्रमा महत्त्वपूर्ण योगदान पुर्याउन नेपाल फर्कनुभयो। उहाँले यसअघि अस्पतालको सञ्चालक समितिको अध्यक्षको रूपमा नेतृत्व गर्नुभएको थियो।

Surgical Specialties

विशेषज्ञ सेवाहरू
