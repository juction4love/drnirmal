Historical Executive Summary

Dr. Nirmal Lamichhane stands as a cornerstone of urological oncology in Nepal. Integrating elite international training from New York and China with local clinical leadership, he has transformed cancer care protocols at the national level.

As a former Chairman and Deputy Director of the B. P. Koirala Memorial Cancer Hospital (BPKMCH), his career is defined by academic precision and surgical innovation.
